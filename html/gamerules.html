Rules of the Game

  • The aim of this game is to destroy as may spaceships as you can within in a span of 90 seconds.

  • Destroying a spaceship involves lining up your canon towards the spaceship and firing at that spaceship to destroy it.
    • Press the left or right arrow key to line up cannon to the spaceship that you want to destroy.
    • Press the spacebar to destroy the spaceship that is in line with canon.

  • Another option to destroy a spaceship without using a keyboard is to click or tap on a UFO.

  • If the spaceship that was destroyed contains the math expression that will equate to the number printed at the cannon, the attack will be a HIT and all the spaceships will move back to the top-most portion of the game screen.

  • If the spaceship that was destroyed contains the math expression that does not equate to the number printed at the cannon, the attack will be a MISS and the spaceships will continue moving towards the canon.

  • The game will be over when either the 90 seconds timer finishes its countdown or when any of the spaceships reaches past the tip of the cannon.

  • The final score of the game will be based on the number of HITS that were recorded during the game.
